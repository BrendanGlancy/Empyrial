By Investors, For Investors.
Multi-approach
From fundamental analysis to quantitative analysis and prediction generation, Empyrial offers a wide range of analysis so you can get the best insight from your investments
AI and data-driven
Empyrial empowers individual and financial institutions whith AI and data so they can tackle the biggest challenges on the financial market
Flexible
Combining multiple librairies and data sources, Empyrial adapts to your needs so you can develop and evolve your strategies faster
Open source
Follow the most open MIT open source protocol, you can get all the project source code on Github, free to use in their own open source projects or commercial projects, and free forever
Features
Empyrial (Engine)
Quantitative portfolio analytics including risks and performance analysis
Oracle
Prediction generation on your portfolio using several prediction models (Prophet, Auto-ARIMA, Fast Fourier Transform...)
Fundlens (fundamental analysis)
Fundamental analysis of each of the assets in your portfolio that gives you the best insight about the true value of a company
Risklens (risk factors analysis)
Risk factors analysis that can give you a clear understanding of how to better manage your portfolio and identify diversification opportunities
Optimizer (portfolio optimization)
Access various optimization methods (Efficient Frontier, Mean-Variance, Black-Litterman, Deep Learning...) and make your portfolio more performant
Sentiment lens (event-driven)
Take advantage of market inefficiencies based on sentiment and news to better identify risks and opportunities
